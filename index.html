FAQ

What can I expect at my first appointment?

You’ll review your medical history, meet with an ENT provider, undergo an exam, and receive diagnostic testing if needed.

What causes snoring?

Snoring occurs when relaxed tissues in the airway vibrate during sleep. It may indicate airway obstruction or sleep apnea.

How can I reduce mild or occasional snoring?

Maintain a healthy weight, avoid alcohol before bedtime, sleep on your side, elevate the head of the bed, and keep consistent sleep habits.

When should I see a doctor for snoring?

If snoring is loud, persistent, disrupts sleep, causes daytime fatigue, or includes choking or gasping episodes.
